Pillars of Eternity


Pillars of Eternity is an isometric RPG set in the fantasy world of Eora, where players embark on a quest to uncover the truth behind a mysterious phenomenon known as the Hollowborn Plague. As the Watcher, players must navigate treacherous lands, confront ancient evils, and make difficult moral choices.

Pillars of Eternity, developed by Obsidian Entertainment, is a modern masterpiece of classic RPG design, offering players a deep and immersive journey through a richly detailed fantasy world. Set in the diverse and intricate setting of Eora, Pillars of Eternity invites players to step into the shoes of the Watcher, a mysterious figure with the ability to perceive and interact with souls. At its core, Pillars of Eternity is a celebration of storytelling, with a richly woven narrative that explores themes of identity, destiny, and the nature of existence. As players journey across the diverse landscapes of Eora, they will encounter a cast of memorable characters, engage in morally complex quests, and confront ancient mysteries that shape the fate of nations. Gameplay in Pillars of Eternity is a seamless blend of real-time exploration and tactical combat, offering players a wealth of options for customizing their party, mastering powerful abilities, and overcoming formidable challenges. Whether engaging in intense battles against fearsome monsters or unraveling the mysteries of ancient ruins, every encounter feels meaningful and rewarding. One of Pillars of Eternity's greatest strengths lies in its deep and immersive world, which reacts to the player's choices and actions in meaningful ways. From the bustling cities of Defiance Bay to the untamed wilderness of the Dyrwood, every location is filled with secrets to uncover, quests to undertake, and characters to meet. While Pillars of Eternity received praise for its rich storytelling, complex characters, and strategic gameplay, some players criticized its steep learning curve and occasional pacing issues. However, these minor flaws do little to detract from the overall experience of immersing oneself in the rich and immersive world of Pillars of Eternity, a must-play for fans of classic RPGs and epic adventures alike.
